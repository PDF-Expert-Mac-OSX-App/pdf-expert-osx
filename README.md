## What is PDF Expert?  

**PDF Expert** is a professional **PDF editing and viewing application** developed to deliver a complete document workflow solution. It allows users to easily view, annotate, modify, convert, and secure PDF files with a streamlined and elegant interface.  

This all-in-one tool is tailored for professionals, students, and business users who frequently handle digital paperwork. You can add notes, highlight text, rearrange pages, fill in interactive forms, or sign documents electronically â€” all without needing external plugins.  

With its powerful editing engine, **PDF Expert** lets you update text, images, and layouts inside existing documents while maintaining original formatting. The integrated **PDF converter** can transform files into editable Word, Excel, or PowerPoint documents with near-perfect accuracy.  

Thanks to macOS optimization and Apple Silicon support, PDF Expert runs efficiently, providing smooth navigation through large documents. It also features **OCR scanning**, enabling users to recognize and edit text from scanned files. With built-in iCloud sync, all your edits stay updated across devices, ensuring a consistent workflow anywhere you go.
